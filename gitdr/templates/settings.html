Encryption active

GitDR uses SQLCipher AES-256 encryption for the database and Fernet symmetric encryption for all credentials and destination configs. The encryption key is derived from your passphrase at startup and never stored on disk.

Database SQLCipher AES-256
Credentials Fernet (AES-128-CBC)
Key Derivation PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA256
⚠ Passphrase rotation
To change your passphrase, stop the service, run gitdr rotate-key, and restart. This re-encrypts all secrets with the new key.
